# E-Commerce-Sales-Analysis
Power BI dashboard analyzing Shopping sales trends and business insights


## About This Project
In this project, I analyzed e-commerce sales data to understand customer behavior, sales trends, and category performance. The goal was to explore how different factors like month, category, gender, and age group impact overall sales and orders.



## Key Highlights
- Total Sales: 251.51M  
- Total Customers: 99,457  
- Total Orders: 99,457  



## Key Insights
- Monthly sales show fluctuations, with some months performing significantly better than others, indicating seasonal demand patterns.
- The **Clothing** category contributes the highest share of total sales, making it the top revenue-generating segment.
- Categories like **Shoes** and **Technology** also perform strongly, while others contribute smaller portions.
- A large portion of orders comes from **Adult customers**, followed by other age groups.
- **Female customers** contribute slightly more to total orders compared to male customers.
- Categories like **Clothing and Cosmetics** have high order volumes, showing strong customer demand.



## My Understanding
From this analysis, I observed that not all categories perform equally—some drive higher revenue while others contribute more to order volume. This means the business should focus on high-performing categories while also improving weaker ones.

Also, customer segmentation (age and gender) plays an important role in understanding buying patterns, which can help in targeted marketing.



## What This Dashboard Covers
- Monthly sales and customer trends  
- Category-wise sales distribution  
- Orders distribution across product categories  
- Gender-wise order comparison  
- Customer segmentation based on age group  
- Interactive filters for better exploration  



## Final Thought
This dashboard shows that understanding customer segments and category performance can help businesses make better decisions, improve marketing strategies, and increase overall sales.
